Talis is considered the sky or the heavens to the residents of Ersos
Talis has three main continents and various islands, most of which are unoccupied. 
Talis has five seas for the four cardinal directions, as well as the Skysea.
The Skysea is the water surrounding the place where Valynor and her followers ascended to Caelum
The Skysea water mirrors the night sky and constellations, even during the day.
